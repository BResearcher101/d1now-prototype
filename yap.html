Young Adult Panel (YAP)

The YAP is a group of young adults living with Type 1 Diabetes, co-designing and shaping the D1 Now study.

Interested in joining?

What is the YAP?

We're a network of young adults with lived experience of Type 1 Diabetes. Co-design is at the heart of the D1 Now study – our voices and experiences help shape the research.

It’s a chance to learn, lead, connect and make real impact.

What does being part of the YAP look like?

Monthly online meetings
Shaping content and communication
Helping with recruitment ideas
Paid for your time and input
“The YAP sessions are laid-back and flexible, I really feel heard and supported.” – YAP member

Want to join the YAP?

We’d love to hear from you. Whether you’re curious, creative, or just want to make change – the YAP is open to you.
